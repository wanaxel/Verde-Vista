Some common questions
were often asked

My flowers are falling off or dying?

Your flowers may be falling off or dying due to issues like insufficient water or inadequate sunlight; identifying the specific problem will help you address and solve it.

What causes leaves to become pale?

Leaves may become pale due to nutrient deficiencies, often caused by poor soil quality or imbalanced fertilizer application. Adjusting nutrient levels in the soil can help restore leaf color.

What causes brown crispy leaves?

Brown, crispy leaves can result from factors like underwatering, excessive sunlight exposure, or low humidity. Adjusting watering frequency and placement can help alleviate the issue.

How do i choose a plant?

Choose a plant by considering factors such as your available space, sunlight conditions, and your commitment to maintenance. Additionally, consider your own preferences regarding the type of plant and its aesthetic appeal.

How do I change the pots?

To change pots, gently remove the plant from its current pot, loosen the roots, and place it in a larger container with fresh, well-draining soil. Water the plant thoroughly after repotting to help it adjust to its new environment.

Why are gnats flying around my plant?

Gnats may be flying around your plant due to overwatering, as the excess moisture creates a favorable environment for fungus gnats. Adjusting your watering routine and allowing the soil to dry out between waterings can help eliminate the gnats.
